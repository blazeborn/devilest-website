The Studio

Focus & Vision

Devilest emerged from a lifelong passion for demon-fighting games like Doom, Devil May Cry, Diablo, and dark fantasy worlds. The studio explores the intersection of technology and spiritual themes, creating games that challenge players both mechanically and philosophically through meaningful action experiences.

 

Founded by Vitor Silvestre, a Brazilian with 10+ years of game development, formal game design education, AAA background, and guitar playing to create meaningful action games with rock n roll attitude.

Current Project

Underworld

Action RPG

Experience the journey of an undead king awakening in the seventh circle of hell. This dark action RPG combines lightning-fast combat with profound spiritual narrative, inspired by Dante's Divine Comedy and Kardecist literature. Navigate through the underworld's treacherous landscapes while mastering devastating abilities and uncovering the truth behind your eternal imprisonment.
